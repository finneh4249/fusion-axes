Fusion Party's Core Values

Six evidence-based principles guiding policy and decision-making

Personal Liberty

Recognising the right of individuals to live their best life and pursue self-actualisation, balanced by safety and ethical conduct. Fusion supports "Positive Liberty" for maximum net freedom.

Advancement

A progressive viewpoint valuing continued application of reason, scientific discovery, and innovation to improve quality of life and solve societal challenges.

Ecological Harmony

Valuing nature intrinsically beyond its use to humans. An ecomodernist approach to reduce environmental impact while enabling high living standards.

Safety

Ensuring all people are safe from violence and deprivation, including provision of necessities like food, shelter, and healthcare with harm minimisation focus.

Ethical Conduct

Commitment to honesty, transparency, and good faith in all actions, especially government. Policies must be evidence-based and systems designed for societal needs.

Equity

Ensuring every person, regardless of background, has fair access to opportunities and government services. A moderating value that contextualises the others.
