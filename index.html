Fusion Party Value Alignment Quiz

What is the Fusion Party Value Alignment Quiz?

This quiz is designed to help you understand how your personal views and priorities align with the core values of the Fusion Party. Fusion Party is a forward-thinking political movement in Australia, built on a foundation of science, evidence, and a commitment to a free, fair, and innovative society.

You will be presented with a series of statements. For each one, simply indicate how much you agree or disagree. Your responses will help create a profile showing your alignment with each of Fusion's six foundational values. Answer honestly to get the most accurate reflection!

There are **48 questions** in total, designed to be thought-provoking yet straightforward for any Australian adult.

What are Fusion Party's Core Values?

The Fusion Party's approach to policy and decision-making is guided by a unique, structured framework of six core values. This quiz will show you how your perspectives relate to each of them:

Personal Liberty: Recognising the right of individuals to live their best life and pursue self-actualisation, balanced by the need for safety and ethical conduct. Fusion supports a "Positive Liberty" philosophy, aiming for maximum net freedom.

Advancement: A progressive viewpoint valuing the continued application of reason, scientific discovery, and innovation to improve quality of life, increase access to resources, and solve societal challenges.

Ecological Harmony: Valuing nature intrinsically, beyond just its use to humans. This involves an ecomodernist approach to reduce environmental impact while enabling high living standards, informed by principles of Deep Ecology.

Safety: Ensuring all people are safe from violence and deprivation, which includes the provision of necessities like food, shelter, and healthcare, and a focus on harm minimisation.

Ethical Conduct: A commitment to honesty, transparency, and good faith in all actions, especially in government. Policies must be evidence-based, and systems designed to align with societal needs.

Equity: Ensuring that every person, regardless of background, has fair and just access to opportunities and services derived from the government. This is a moderating value that contextualises the others.

Why Take This Quiz?

Understanding your alignment with a political party's core values can be more insightful than just looking at individual policies. This quiz aims to:

  • Provide you with a deeper understanding of Fusion Party's foundational principles.
  • Help you reflect on your own political and social perspectives.
  • Offer a starting point for considering how your vote can best represent your views.

At the end, you'll see your alignment with each of the six Fusion Values and receive a suggestion for how you might consider preferencing candidates in your electorate, based on your responses. You'll also have the option to discuss your results further with our AI guide!
