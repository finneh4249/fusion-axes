Be Honest

For the most accurate results, answer based on your genuine beliefs and values, not what you think sounds "correct" or socially desirable.

Response Options

You'll have five response options for each statement:

Strongly Agree You completely agree with the statement
Agree You mostly agree with the statement
Neutral/Unsure You're undecided or the statement doesn't apply
Disagree You mostly disagree with the statement
Strongly Disagree You completely disagree with the statement
